Pastor Olajide Olanrewaju

A visionary leader, Pastor Olajide Olanrewaju stands at the forefront of ALIVE FOR CHRIST with a mission deeply rooted in revival, purity, and transformation. With over two decades of ministerial experience, he bridges timeless scripture with modern-day impact—leading a global community into intimacy with God through prayer, truth, and supernatural encounters.

Known for his prophetic insight and compassionate heart, Pastor Olajide Olanrewaju believes the next great move of God will be through surrendered people walking in kingdom identity. Under his leadership, the church has flourished into a hub of restoration and power, equipping both young and old to shine boldly for Christ in all spheres of influence.

When not behind the pulpit, he is a devoted husband, father, author, and mentor—raising a generation ready to walk in purpose, healing, and holiness. His motto remains: "Revive the earth, one soul at a time."
